Services

As a dedicated and accomplished speedcuber, I offer a range of services to help you improve your cubing skills, whether you're a beginner looking to solve your first Rubik's Cube or an experienced solver aiming to break your personal best. With personalized coaching, interactive workshops, and insightful resources, I am here to assist you on your speedcubing journey.

  1. Speedcubing Workshops: Join interactive group workshops designed to cover various aspects of speedcubing, including solving methods, finger techniques, advanced algorithms, and solving under pressure. These workshops provide an opportunity to learn from fellow cubers, ask questions, and receive immediate feedback.
  2. Solving Methods:
    • Beginner's Method
    • CFOP (Fridrich Method)
    • Roux Method
    • ZZ Method (Zbigniew Zborowski Method)
    • Petrus Method
    • Heise Method
    • Waterman Method
    • Guimond Method
    • CLL (Corners of the Last Layer)
    • EG (Edge Orientation and Permutation)
  3. Hand Dexterity and Coordination: Here I will guide you through finger tricks, teaching exercises and drills to strengthen your fingers and enhance coordination. We will focus on gradual progression and provide valuable tips to improve lookahead and fluidity of movements. With also flexible schedules, practice materials, and ongoing support, you will achieve faster solve times and excel in speedcubing.
  4. Algorithm Database and Resources: Access a comprehensive database of algorithms for various solving methods, including CFOP, Roux, and ZZ. Additionally, I provide written and video tutorials explaining advanced techniques, finger tricks, and efficient solving approaches.
  5. Competition Preparation: If you're gearing up for a speedcubing competition, I can provide specialized training and guidance to help you perform at your best. I'll assist you in optimizing your solve times, fine-tuning your algorithms, and developing effective strategies to handle competition pressure.
  6. Feedback and Analysis: Send in your solving videos for detailed analysis and feedback. I'll review your techniques, execution, and suggest areas for improvement. This service is valuable for identifying any inefficiencies in your solves and fine-tuning your approach.
  7. Online Support: Reach out to me for quick questions, advice, or troubleshooting via email or a dedicated chat platform. I'm here to support you on your speedcubing journey whenever you need assistance.
